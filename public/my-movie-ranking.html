Best Movie According Phong Le

My top 3 moive of this year

Les Misérable (2012)

Set in France during the early nineteenth century,
the film tells the story of Jean Valjean who, while being hunted for decades by the ruthless policeman Javert after breaking parole,
agrees to care for a factory worker's daughter. The story reaches resolution against the background of the June Rebellion of 1832.

The Glory (2023)

“The Glory” is a Netflix series depicting the story of a woman whose soul was broken by violence as a child,
and the desperate revenge that she carefully prepared throughout her whole life and those who fall into her vortex.
The leads in the drama are Song HyeKyo and Lee DoHyun

Prison playbook (2018)

Synopsis. A captivating series revolving around the lives of convicts behind bars,
their families and duty officers working in the correctional facilities. It focuses on the story of a superstar baseball pitcher,
Kim Je-hyuk who is convicted of assault after he saves his sister from a sexual assault.
